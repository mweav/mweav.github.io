    Where should you study?

    By 34th Street Magazine
    Which school are you in?
    1. College of Arts and Sciences.
    2. The Wharton School.
    3. Nursing.
    4. Engineering.
    5. You don't even go here. (or you're a dual degree — you break those barriers!)
    What is your preferred noise level?
    1. Silent.
    2. Pretty quiet.
    3. You don't mind background noise.
    4. Some conversations is OK.
    5. You're feeling chatty.
    Do you like to eat while you study?
    1. No food whatsoever.
    2. I like the option if I'm hungry.
    3. I frequently get POD delivery
    4. No, but other people can.
    5. Yes, I require copious snack breaks.
    Are you alone or with friends?
    1. I always act alone.
    2. Some friends might come and meet me later.
    3. I'm with a buddy.
    4. With friends but all working independently.
    5. Group project!
    How many bathroom breaks do you take?
    1. I'm not moving until this is done.
    2. A few.
    3. Maybe one or two, but only if it's clean.
    4. That's a weird question...
    5. After all that coffee? Quite a few.
    How many hours are you staying for.
    1. I have a few hours to kill.
    2. This is an all day commitment
    3. This might take a while, I want to feel at home.
    4. It's going to be a late night.
    5. Quick study sesh between classes.
    What side of campus are you on?
    1. Near the Quad.
    2. Near DRL (why, though?)
    3. Middle of campus.
    4. Near Beige Block
    5. Passing the Compus.
